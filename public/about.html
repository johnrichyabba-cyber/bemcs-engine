About the System
Core purpose and control environment

Bandari Electronic Monitoring and Control System (BE-MCs) is designed to support real-time port intelligence, customs visibility, shipment monitoring, and operational control.

The Tracking module is used to fetch cargo movement intelligence and approval state from trusted sources. Once approved, shipments can be transferred into Shipment Registry to open operational shipment files.

The system also includes Accounting System and System Health modules to support financial visibility, invoice control, collection monitoring, and service readiness.
